The place for new ideas

The Cartland Convention Center's breathtaking design welcomes travelers of all markets into a comfortable setting of beautifully rich architecture.

The newest and most modern Convention center Cartland spans 2200 square meters and offers a well planned and equipped complex of 5 halls with a combined seating capacity of 1200 seats. One underground and one overground parking and additional entrance for large decor pieces and equipment. Catering available from two restaurants. The halls can be used for conferences, seminars, meetings, entertainment events, presentations, weddings, and others. Four of the five halls can host training sessions and other events connected to physical activities.
